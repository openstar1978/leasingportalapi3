a. If you or anyone who guaranteed the Agreement:
(i) fail to pay any Rental or any other sum due under this Agreement or any other agreement with us within 3 days of our demanding payment or;
(ii) do not keep to any of the terms of this Agreement or any other agreement with us after we have given not less than 10 days to remedy the breach, if we think that the breach is remediable; then in every such case an “event of default” will be deemed to have occurred.
